The Record Player Song

by Daisy the Great

Kelly Dugan and Mina Walker met as acting majors at NYU's Tisch School of the Arts, becoming friends and forming their band Daisy the Great in 2016. The Record Player Song was released on June 21st, 2017, as their debut single. It later appeared on their first EP I've Got a Few Friends & I Wish They Were Mine, realeased on January 19th, 2018. In early 2021, The Record Player Song became a hit on Tiktok, bringing more attention to their music and leading to a collaboration with popular indie-pop band AJR.
